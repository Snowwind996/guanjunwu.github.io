About Me

I am an undergraduate student majoring in computer science and minoring in economics at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Under the supervised of Prof. Xinggang Wang with and the guidance of Jiemin Fang in the School of Electronic Information and Communications, I will begin my PhD career in collaboration with HuaWei in September this year. My research interests mainly focus on computer vision, with a specific focus on Neural Rendering.

Additionally, I have previously interned with ByteDance on the User Growth team, where my primary responsibilities involved developing GPH (Generalized Propensity Hazard) models and causal forest population models. I also contributed to the development of perception models for autonomous driving at SenseTime.

Currently, I am dedicated to addressing challenges in Neural Radiance Fields. Please feel free to contact me via email.
